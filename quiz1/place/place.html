Wander & Wonder!

Tourist spots that feel like Shaun and the flock’s playground—fun, lively, and always ready for a little adventure 💫

Kayangan Api

Kayangan Api is an eternal flame located in the protected forest of Sendangharjo Village, Ngasem District, Bojonegoro, East Java. This natural geological wonder happens when underground gas escapes and ignites, creating flames that never go out, even in the rain, making it a seriously epic sight to see. 🔥🌳🐑

Masjid Nahda

The charm of this mosque comes from its grand and unique architecture, making visitors feel like they’ve stepped into Persia. An-Nahda Mosque shines in multiple golden domes and towering minarets. Detailed carvings blend Arab and Javanese cultures, giving the mosque an extra majestic and aesthetic vibe. 🕌✨🐑

Growgoland

At Growgoland, you can totally dive into crystal-clear spring water, chill under shady trees, and vibe with nature all around. Plus, snack vendors and floatie rentals are everywhere, so you can snack, float, and just live your best day out. No stress, all fun, totally fresh! 🐑💦

Starfruit Garden

At Ngringinrejo Starfruit Garden, you don’t just walk around—you pick and taste fresh belimbing straight from the tree, join fun garden tours, explore kiddo zones, and grab starfruit snacks to take home. All the fun, all the freshness, and totally free! 🍊🐑
