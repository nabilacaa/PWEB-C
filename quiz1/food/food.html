Yummy, Yummy, in My Tummy!

Just like Shaun and the flock sneaking snacks in the meadow, playful and too good to resist. 🌿🍪

Bojonegoro may not be famous for a wide range of original dishes, but that doesn’t mean we’re out of flavor! Instead, we’ve got some tasty local foods that are adapted and reimagined from nearby towns. It’s kinda like Shaun and the flock borrowing ideas for a new snack—familiar, but with a Bojonegoro twist that makes it feel like home.” 🐑🍴

Ledre

Ledre is a snack made from plantains that has a sweet taste and crunchy texture. Ledre has a distinctive flavor, which is a combination of the natural sweetness of plantains and the savory taste of coconut milk. Its texture is crunchy and light, making it perfect as a snack to accompany tea or coffee.

Sego Buwuhan

Sego buwuhan is white rice with various side dishes such as beef, eggs, blonceng vegetables, fried chili sauce, yellow noodles, tempe bobor, and rempeyek, which are quite varied, with a distinctive thick coconut milk sauce, wrapped in leaves to add to the deliciousness of this dish when enjoyed.
